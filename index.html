Meet Your Stylist

Born and raised in Manila, Philippines, Katrina graduated from Paul Mitchell The School in Milwaukee in 2012 and built Kookai Hair Salon on one idea: color should grow out as beautifully as it goes on.

The salon focuses on low-maintenance, natural-looking color and precision cuts — but if you ask Katrina what matters most, it's simple: she wants every client to feel at home and enjoy every moment of the service. That warmth spills into every corner of the space, too — and if the decor didn't give it away, she loves all things pink and Hello Kitty.
